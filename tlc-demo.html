Traffic Light Controller — FSM Live Demo

A 6-state Moore machine for a highway / farm-road intersection, running the same logic as the Verilog RTL. Highway has priority; the farm road only gets a green when a car is waiting.
Signal encoding (matches the RTL): 2'b01 = red · 2'b10 = yellow · 2'b11 = green. The farm road stays red until you press Farm car waiting.
